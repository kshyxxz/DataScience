Pancakes
Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Baking Powder
Instructions: Mix all the ingredients together until smooth. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.

Salad
Ingredients: Lettuce, Tomatoes, Cucumbers, Olive Oil, Lemon Juice, Salt, Pepper
Instructions: Wash and chop the lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

Smoothie
Ingredients: Bananas, Strawberries, Yogurt, Honey, Ice Cubes
Instructions: Combine bananas, strawberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes in a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
